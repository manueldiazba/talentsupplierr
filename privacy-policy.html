Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how TalentSupplier ("we," "us") handles personal information in connection with this website and our executive search services.

Who we are

TalentSupplier is an executive search boutique. Contact: info@talentsupplier.eu.

Information we collect

We collect information you provide directly — for example your name, email address and any details you share when making an enquiry or submitting your profile for a search.

How we use it

We use this information to respond to enquiries, to carry out executive search and recruitment services, and to communicate with clients and candidates. We do not sell personal information to third parties.

Confidentiality

Candidate and client information is handled confidentially and shared only as necessary to progress a specific search, and only with relevant parties' knowledge.

Cookies

This website does not use tracking or advertising cookies.

Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, delete or restrict the use of your personal information. To exercise these rights, contact info@talentsupplier.eu.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to info@talentsupplier.eu.
